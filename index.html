Cristiano Ronaldo

The best of the world

Here's a time line of Cristiano Ronaldo:

  • 1985 - Born in Funchal, Ilha da Madeira, Portugal
  • 1995 - He went to Clube Desportivo Nacional and his success with the team led him to sign with Sporting Clube de Portugal for two seasons and managed to become the only player to have played for Sporting under-16, under-17, under-18, B, and in the first team, all in one season.
  • 2003 - Manchester United signed him in 2003, for €15 million,[27] and renewed the contract on April 13, 2007 until 2012.[28][29][30] He asked for number 28 (his number at Sporting) as he didn't want the pressure of living up to the expectation attached to the number 7 jersey, which had already been worn by players like George Best, Bryan Robson, Éric Cantona and David Beckham . “After I walked in, the manager asked me what number I'd like. I said 28. But Ferguson said, 'No, you're going to get number 7,' and the famous shirt was an extra source of motivation. They were forced to live up to an honor".
  • 2006 - The 2006–2007 season proved Ronaldo's year, as he broke the 20 goal barrier for the first time and achieved his first league title with Manchester United. In November and December 2006, Ronaldo received consecutive Barclays Premier League awards, becoming only the third player in Premier League history to win titles after Dennis Bergkamp in 1997 and Robbie Fowler in 1996.[34][35] He scored his 50th goal for Manchester United against rivals Manchester City on May 5, 2007 as United won his first Premier League title in four years, and he was voted for his second consecutive FIFPro Player Special Youth of the Year, at the end of the year.
  • 2008 - In the 2007–08 Champions League final on May 21 against rivals Chelsea, Ronaldo scored the first goal after 26 minutes, which was negated by a Chelsea draw in the 45th minute when the game ended 1– 1, after extension. His misfire on penalties put Chelsea in a position to win the trophy, but John Terry beat the penalty by slipping; with that United were victorious 6–5 on penalties. Ronaldo was named by fans the UEFA Man of the Game, and ended the campaign with a career-high 42 goals counting in all competitions, dropping four shorts from Denis Law's team record 46 in the 1963–1964 season. In the Champions League, he was also named best striker and player at the tournament and was the competition's top scorer.
  • 2009 - Real Madrid reached an agreement with Manchester United for the transfer of the then best player in the world in 2009, for the announced amount of 94 million euros on 11 June 2009. The player thus became the most expensive contract in the history of world football until then, surpassing the values of the contracting of Zinédine Zidane.
  • 2011 - Ronaldo became the top scorer in a season in Real Madrid history with 53 goals, beating Ferenc Puskás' previous record of 49 goals. Ronaldo broke the record for most goals scored in a season in the League.
  • 2013 - He was awarded the FIFA Golden Ball for the second time in his life and, once again, he was voted the best player in the world, he would put an end to the Argentine Lionel Messi's succession. Ronaldo was moved to receive the award that was given by the former player Pelé.
  • 2015 - He was awarded the 2014 FIFA Ballon d'Or for the third time in his career, beating German goalkeeper Manuel Neuer and Argentine Lionel Messi.
  • 2016 - For the season and fantastic year of the Portuguese star's career, he received for the fourth time in his career the award for best player in the world, the Ballon d'Or, which was once again presented by France Football magazine.
  • 2017 - He was chosen for the fourth time by FIFA as the best player in the world, beating Frenchman Antoine Griezmann and Argentine Lionel Messi. Contrary to what happened in other editions, in this one FIFA opened to public voting, and was called The Best for the first time, also because of the Ballon d'Or award, once again being presented by France Football magazine.
  • 2018 - He won his fourth Champions League for the meringues (third in a row), his fifth in his career and his seventh artillery in the competition (sixth in a row) and Cristiano Ronaldo signed by Juventus for €100m on a four-season contract. With that, it became the biggest transfer to an Italian club in history.
  • 2019 - It was the inaugural final of the international football competition involving the men's teams of the UEFA member associations. The game took place on June 9, 2019, at Estádio do Dragão, in Porto, Portugal, and was played by the hosts Portugal and the Netherlands. Portugal won the final 1-0 and became the first UEFA League of Nations champion.
  • 2021 - Cristiano Ronaldo became the first and only player in history to be top scorer in three of the top five European leagues, as well as being voted by fans once again the player of the year for Juventus. In all, he scored 36 goals during the season, Cristiano Ronaldo returned to Manchester United after 12 years and became the top scorer of national teams in history alone, with 111 goals scored.
